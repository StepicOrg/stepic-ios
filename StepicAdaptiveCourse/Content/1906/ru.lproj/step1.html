GMAT Data Sufficiency Problems

This adaptive course can be used for preparation to the Data Sufficiency section of the GMAT test. Each problem consists of a question and two statements, labeled (1) and (2), in which certain data are given. You have to decide whether the data given in the statements are sufficient for answering the question.
